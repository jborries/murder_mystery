FW Murder Mystery at the Amon G. Carter Museum:

A Murder Most Foul

The crime scene has been cordoned off. The witnesses interviewed. The suspects corralled. Traces of smoke linger in the air as the facts are sorted out. The only thing missing is you.

Join a cadre amateur sleuths, femme fatales, and private dicks at the Amon G. Carter Museum for a sultry night of mystery and suspense...all in benefit of the Big Brothers Big Sisters Program.

Explore the halls of the museum to find our Murder Mystery players.

Ask them questions. Check the facts. Find the killer.

If you have the moxie to discover the murderer, you may just be selected as the winner...along with a handsome reward.



The theme is Film Noir and we highly encourage everyone to dress the part. We have secured discounts at several local costume shops to help set the atmosphere. You can find inspiration and the discounts here. Sure, you don't have to dress up, but you don't want to be that guy.

So save the date or buy Tickets now. Enjoy free food, free beer, and discounted cigars from our generous sponsors Abuelo's, Hop Fusion Ale Works, and Silver Leaf Cigar Lounge. Our friends from Thompson's will also be on site slinging drinks if you need something a little stronger.

Online ticket sales are limited in number. Once they're gone, you're s.o.l. friend.
